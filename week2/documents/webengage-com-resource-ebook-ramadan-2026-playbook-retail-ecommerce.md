Ramadan 2026: Playbook for SEA Retail & E-commerce
Ramadan shifts behaviour. Your execution needs to shift, too.
During Ramadan, routines compress, attention windows tighten, and purchase decisions shift later in the day. Campaigns often underperform because teams can’t adapt execution fast enough at scale.
Inside this eBook
How Ramadan 2026 reshapes shopping across SEA
– shorter windows, repeat visits, and later decisions.
The post-Iftar peak window
and how to plan engagement around it.
The 5 execution problems that break most Ramadan campaigns
– segmentation, timing, catalog personalisation, channel coordination, and monitoring at scale.
A practical framework for:
segmentation, timing, cart recovery, smarter incentives, catalog recommendations, faster launches, and real-time optimisation
A 4-phase Ramadan execution framework
– pre-Ramadan → early → mid → final days
