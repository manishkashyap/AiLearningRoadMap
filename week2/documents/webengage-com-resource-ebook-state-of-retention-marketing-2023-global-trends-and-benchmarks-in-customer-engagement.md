State Of Retention Marketing
spotlights latent customer engagement trends in 2023 across different channels, industries, and geographies.
These trends can be a wayfinder for those who wish to master the art of retaining customers and maximizing revenue.
Information is power. Part of being a brand means weathering macroeconomic conditions and recalibrating strategies to not only make an impact but also a profit.
Without information, you are blindsided by unforeseeable factors like financial upheavals and geopolitical uncertainties. Even if you manage to leverage second or third-party data like cookies and data brokers, they may be low quality, poorly aggregated, bound by red tape, or not work well with your system architecture.
