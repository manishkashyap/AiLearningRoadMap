Limitations of Segment.com for Integrating Hybrid Apps
Our Segment.com integration works only for native iOS and Android apps. It cannot be used for integrating mobile apps built on
React Native, Flutter, Xamarin
and
Unity.
You can choose to integrate your platforms with WebEngage via Segment.com through a
Basic Integration
or
Advanced Integration
, as per your business needs.
Basic Integration
(Referred to as as
Cloud Mode
on Segment.com)
Basic Integration
between
WebEngage
and
Segment
can be configured in less than a minute. It enables you to:
Receive
user data
and
events data
from your website, native Android & iOS apps in your WebEngage account.
Engage users through
SMS, On-site, Web Push, Email, WhatsApp,
and retarget them through
Facebook and Google Adwords.
However, with
Basic Integration
you will not be able to engage app users through
Push Notifications
and
In-app Messages.
Advanced Integration
(Referred to as
Device Mode
on Segment.com)
Advanced Integration
requires you to modify your platform SDK to include WebEngage’s library for Segment. Doing so enables you to:
Receive
user data
and
events data
from all your sources, website, native Android & iOS apps, and server in your WebEngage account.
Engage users through all the channels of engagement including
Push, In-app, SMS, On-site, Web Push, Email, WhatsApp,
and retarget them through
Facebook and Google Adwords.
