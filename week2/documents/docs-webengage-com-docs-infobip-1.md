Configuring WhatsApp Service Provider
Following are the steps that will enable you to add the WhatsApp Service Provider to the list of WSPs offered by WebEngage.
Step 1: Add WhatsApp Service Provider (WSP)
Click the Add WhatsApp Service Provider (WSP) Button/ Create (Plus) icon placed on the top. In doing so, you will be prompted by a configuration modal. Select
Infobip
from the first dropdown,
WhatsApp Service Provider.
Step 2: Name your Configuration
Please add a user-friendly name that enables you to identify the appropriate WSP for a campaign while creating it. This comes in handy, especially when you have multiple accounts with the same WSP.
Step 3: Add WhatsApp Business Number
Please specify the verified phone number that you'll be using to send all your
WhatsApp campaigns
in this field.
Step 4: Add API Key
🚧
Here's how you can generate a new API Key for integrating WebEngage
Navigate to your
Infobip dashboard
to find your
publicApiKey
, add it in the configuration modal and click
Add WSP.
Please connect with your
Infobip Account Manager
for further assistance.
Step 5: Add WebEngage Webhook URL in Infobip Dashboard**
(Highly Recommended)
Adding the
WebEngage Webhook
in
Infobip
will enable us to receive delivery status notifications for each user. This includes
campaign performance indicators
like message delivered, failed, and queued.
Here's how you can go about it:
Step 5.1.:
As shown below, you will find your
Infobip integration
under the section,
Your WhatsApp Service Provider List.
Click to enlarge
Step 5.2.:
Click the overflow menu placed on the extreme right, click
View Webhook URL
, and copy the link.
Step 5.3.:
Paste the
WebEngage Webhook URL
under the relevant field in your
Infobip dashboard
and click
Save.
Please connect with your
Infobip Account Manager
for further assistance.
WhatsApp Templates
WhatsApp templates can be easily created by following the below steps:
Step 1: Getting Started
Click on the Plus icon next to WhatsApp Templates to get started.
Step 2: Select the WSP
Select
Infobip
from the drop-down menu to send the campaign.
Step 3: Add Template Details
Template Name
(Paste the exact name that has been approved).
Template Text
(Paste the exact message that has been approved, do no edit).
Template Namespace
(While copying from your Facebook/WSP dashboard, please ensure that you use the format,
<template_namespace>:<template_name>
, for namespace)
Template Language
(
List of languages
supported by WhatsApp).
Please get in touch with your
WSP Account Manager
or
Facebook business support
if you're unable to find these details.
Step 4: Click Add Template
Your template is now saved and ready to send your campaign.
