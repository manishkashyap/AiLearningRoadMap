Modifying Funnels
A step-by-step guide on how to modify funnels in your dashboard
Within the new UI, you can modify the funnel by 3 ways,
Edit
,
Copy
, or
Delete
.
Editing a Funnel
All funnels can be modified through two sections in your dashboard;
By clicking on the
Edit
option within the three dots adjacent to the funnel you want to modify. OR
You can directly enter the funnel and modify the section you want.
Here's a brief explanation of how you can access these sections:
The first option is by clicking on the edit option in the 3 dots adjacent to the funnel you want to modify, which then open to the funnel page where you can modify all the aspects of your funnel, as you want.
The second option to edit funnels is by clicking on the funnel name, which then takes you to the funnels page, where you can edit or modify sections of the funnel.
Copy or Duplicate Funnel
This feature comes in handy when creating funnels to track variations of a predefined path. This variation is usually caused in places where the user can choose between performing two different actions at the same step. Let's demonstrate with an example.
👍
Use-case: Duplicating funnel for tracking checkout with a different payment options
Let’s consider a checkout flow where the user has an option of selecting two payment methods -
Cash on Delivery
and
Online Payment.
While the events performed by all the users will be the same, the event attributes for the step,
Checkout - Payment Added
will differ as follows;
Checkout funnel 1
Event:
Checkout - Payment Added
Event attribute 1:
Payment Method
equal to
Cash on Delivery
Variation in checkout funnel 2
Event:
Checkout - Payment Added
Event attribute 2:
Payment Method
equal to
Online Payment
Thus, we can simply create
funnel 1
and duplicate it to the accommodate the variation in event attributes of the fifth step;
Checkout - Payment Added.
This way we will have the following funnels in half the time.
