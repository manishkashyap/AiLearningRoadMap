WhatsApp Template Media: Sizing Guide
Documenting the sizing guide for your WhatsApp templates.
When sending messages through the WhatsApp templates, it's a good idea to attach an image to the header of your template message. Adding an image can effectively capture your customers' attention and visually communicate your product, proposal, or offer.
But what are the optimal dimensions for an image sent via WhatsApp templates?
While you can technically send images with any aspect ratio, such as 1:1 or 16:9, and they will be delivered successfully, but there's a catch. In some cases, customers might need to tap on the image to view it in fullscreen to see the entire image clearly.
