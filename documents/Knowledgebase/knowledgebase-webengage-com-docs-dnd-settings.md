DND Hours
A step-by-step guide on how to set up DND hours for your account
Specifying
Do Not Disturb (DND) Hours
for your account allows you to manage user engagement across channels and journeys. It's a simple one-time setup that helps you prevent users from receiving campaigns during specific periods, such as when they may be asleep or non-serviceable hours of your business. The best part - these settings are applied to a user as per their current timezone!
🚧
Please Note
This setting can be configured by only those
Admins
that have access to
Account Management.
If you are unable to configure, then please get in touch with the account owner.

How to Set up DND Hours
You can choose to specify do not disturb hours for various days of the week. This will ensure that none of your users receive campaigns (for which you have enabled DND hours), as and when the specified time slot occurs in their timezone.

Adding Multiple DND Time Slots
Depending on your user's preferences and behavior, you can choose to define multiple time slots for various days of the week. All you need to do is:
Click the
Add New DND Time Slot
button, as shown below.
Doing so will add a new row to the screen, allowing you to define an additional time slot.
Click to enlarge
Follow the steps discussed above to specify day(s) and duration.
Repeat to configure additional time slots and hit
Save!
If you have set up multiple DND time slots, then campaign delivery will be restricted for a combination of all the time slots, for the respective days.

A Few Things to Keep in Mind
This setting can be disabled for a campaign or journey while creating it. If disabled, then the respective campaigns will be delivered even during
DND hours.
All campaigns sent with
DND enabled
during a user's
DND hours,
will either fail or get queued as per the individual campaign's
queueing settings
.
DND hours
cannot be applied to
In-app
and
On-site
campaigns. Given the targeted nature of these channels, campaigns are sent only in the context of a user's actions on your app or website, rendering such restrictions unnecessary.
Please refer to
DND Hours
and
Queueing
for a detailed understanding of how you can leverage this feature for maximum impact.
