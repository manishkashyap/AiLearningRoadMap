On-site Notifications
Welcome to WebEngage's guide to crafting impactful web overlays!
Engaging site visitors with highly targeted and personalized messages is one of the most effective ways to build trust. This is why your dashboard enables you to create dynamic
On-site Notifications
and control their appearance, visibility as per your needs.
You can leverage ready-to-use templates to have notifications up-and-running for all desktop and mobile users within minutes! You can also choose to build custom notifications from scratch by adding your code and brand guidelines to deliver seamless web experiences.
So, Let's get you started!
Step 1:
Enable
Notifications
under
Data Platform > Integrations > Channels > On-site
in your dashboard.
(No additional configurations needed)
Step 2:
Create your
Onsite Notification
and deliver highly tailored website experiences!

Team up with your tech team to
implement additional customizations via our Web SDK

for multiple notifications on your site.

on driving user engagement and conversions.

On-site Notification
