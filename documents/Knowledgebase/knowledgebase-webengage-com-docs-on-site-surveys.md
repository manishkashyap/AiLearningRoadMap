On-site Surveys
Welcome to WebEngage's guide to collective crucial user data via contextual surveys!
While behavioral data is a great indicator of how your users interact with your site, their immediate needs, and preference, it can't be a spokesperson for what they think or feel.
Surveys
enable you to bridge this gap by collecting actual user responses to pressing questions like:
Do they like the new features/content you've added to your platform?
Which products/services are they likely to purchase and why?
What do they love the most about your brand?
How likely are they to recommend your brand to their friends and family?
- and much more!
Your dashboard enables you to create on-site (and off-site) surveys with great ease. You can leverage ready-to-use templates to have surveys up-and-running for all desktop and mobile users within minutes. You can also build intuitive question sequences based on a user's answer to the previous question.
So, Let's get you started!
