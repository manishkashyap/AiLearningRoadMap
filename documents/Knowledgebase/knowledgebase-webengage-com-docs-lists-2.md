Lists
Static Lists
are the perfect solution for executing one-off campaigns for a small group of users. The term
static
refers to the fact that once created, the user pool remains stagnant and is not updated to factor in your users' changing preferences, traits, and latest platform/channel interactions.
Thus, these are ideal for running one-off campaigns where your end goal is to invoke immediate action like user activation, conversion, registration, or convey a personalized message.
You can also think of these as time capsules that capture user groups and help you preserve their data. This comes in handy when you need to collate specific
User IDs
for identifying important user groups. Additionally, you can also upload a list of
User IDs
to create a
Static List
and subsequently engage those users with relevant campaigns.
How It Works
You can engage the user pool of a
Static List
with highly personalized
One-time
,
Triggered
,
and
Recurring campaigns
through
Push
,
SMS
,
Web Push
,
Email
, and
WhatsApp
.
Static Lists
are not ideal for contextual engagement, and thus, you cannot leverage them to deliver personalized platform experiences via
On-site Notifications
,
In-app Overlays
,
and
Inline (Web) Personalization campaigns.
Given their unchanging nature,
Static User Lists
lack real-time context and cannot be used to run
Relays
or deliver
Transactional Campaigns
.
Their usage is heavily restricted for building
Journeys
and can be used only for triggering a Journey experience for
users who are already in the Segment.
Currently, you cannot export
Static Lists
to your
Facebook Business
and
Google Adwords
accounts to run targeted ad campaigns. We'll soon make this possible for you!
