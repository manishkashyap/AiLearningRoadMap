The Power of WhatsApp
To put things into perspective, here's why this new channel deserves to be an integral part of your engagement strategy:
1. Most Preferred Channel of Engagement:
With over 1.5 billion users worldwide and
60 billion messages sent per day
, WhatsApp has revolutionized the way we communicate. This makes it the perfect platform to connect with users.
2. Interactive Communication:
Modern customers want to interact with a business just like they communicate with friends and family. WhatsApp enables you to provide the same comfort with secured, informal chats that feel just like face-to-face conversations.
3. Unparalleled Brand Recognition:
You can easily leverage WhatsApp to reinforce your brand identity by creating a stunning business profile and providing company details like phone number, address, website/social media links. Coupled with contextual campaigns and thoughtful conversations, you can make your business stand out!
4. Unrestricted Accessibility:
WhatsApp provides personal and easy access to a user's life. It opens up a two-way channel of communication that enables users and businesses alike to connect whenever needed. Users can respond to your WhatsApp campaigns and even initiate a chat through the Customer Service Window - it's every digital business's dream come true!
