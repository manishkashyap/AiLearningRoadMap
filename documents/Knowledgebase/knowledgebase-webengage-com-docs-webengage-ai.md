WebEngage’s AI Capabilities Today & The Roadmap Ahead
Executive Summary
Turn customer behavior into measurable outcomes by combining predictive signals, workflow-accelerating agents, and outcome-driven automation - all under your control.
AI at WebEngage helps teams move faster and make smarter choices. We split AI capabilities into these three clear layers:
Optimize
Provides per-user and per-cohort predictions (time, channel, likelihoods), scores, and continuous path optimizers.
Assist
Assistive Agents that convert business intent into production-ready segments, journeys, and creative assets, reducing setup time and mistakes.
Act
WebEngage BLACK
Outcome-driven playbooks that execute, optimize, and measure automatically within guardrails.

Best Time
Predicts the best time slot to send a campaign to each user based on recent engagement patterns, timezone, and historical responsiveness. By scheduling sends when individuals are most likely to open or engage, overall deliverability and conversion improve.

Insight Agent
Identifies the top factors moving a funnel metric or campaign KPI up or down and surfaces concise explanations (e.g., user cohorts, content variants, etc.). Leverages AI to automatically add smart tags by checking your campaign content.

Segment Agent
Build customer segments using natural language, not query syntax. Stop guessing which attributes to choose - just tell the agent what you need, and it builds the segment for you.
Journey Agent
Create or modify multi-step journeys through conversational prompts. The agent outputs a draft journey that can be reviewed, tweaked, and published. Automatically follows best practices learnt over several years.
Content Agent
Eliminate content bottlenecks. Auto-create on-brand copy and creatives, with brand rules learned from your site, PDFs, and past campaigns. Comes with strong guardrails and easy image-editing.
