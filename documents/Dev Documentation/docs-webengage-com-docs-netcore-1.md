Channel Configuration
SMS
Netcore
Follow the below steps to configure
Netcore
as your SMS Service Provider (SSP).
Click to enlarge
As shown above:
Step 1: Select SSP
Select
Netcore
from the List of Available SSPs. In doing so, you will be prompted by a configuration modal.
Step 2: Name your Configuration
Please add a user-friendly name that enables you to identify the right SSP for a campaign
while creating it
. This comes in handy especially, when you have multiple accounts with the same SSP for sending different types of campaigns.
For example, if you have chosen to use
Netcore
for sending both
Transactional and Promotional campaigns
, then we recommend that you indicate the same by naming the configuration something like:
Netcore Global a/c
Netcore (all campaigns)
Step 3: Add Feed ID
You will need to contact your relationship manager at
Netcore
to obtain your unique
Feed ID.
Step 4: Add Username and Password
Navigate to your
Netcore dashboard
to find and copy your account details. Paste it in the configuration modal, click
Add SSP
, and you're good to go!
Step 5: Add WebEngage Webhook URL in Netcore Dashboard
(Highly Recommended)
Adding the
WebEngage Webhook
in
Netcore
will enable us to receive delivery status notifications for each user. This includes
campaign performance indicators
like message delivered, failed, and queued.
Here's how you can go about it:
Step 5.1.:
As shown below, you will find your
Netcore integration
under the section,
Your SMS Service Provider List.
Click to enlarge
Step 5.2.:
Click the overflow menu placed on the extreme right, click
View Webhook URL
, and copy it.
Step 5.3.:
Navigate to your
Netcore dashboard
and paste the
WebEngage Webhook
under the field,
Realtime Delivery URL.
Additionally, you will need to configure the following details under this field:
Transaction ID
Mobile No.
Delivery Status
Step 5.4.:
Request
Netcore
to disable the feature -
Duplicate Message Checking
for your account.
