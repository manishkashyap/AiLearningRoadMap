What is a CDP?
A Customer Data Platform (CDP) is a packaged software that aggregates and unifies customer data from various sources to provide a 360-degree view of a user.
Why do we need CDPs?
Data is the most valuable resource. Because of the copious influx of data in our systems and the dissipation of third-party data like cookies, brands have been investing heavily in first-party data to acquire authentic, company-specific, and invaluable information about their users.
In acquiring first-party data, brands often struggle to collect, unify, and orchestrate data for marketing campaigns due to issues stemming from siloed systems, lack of integrations, and inability to consolidate user-specific data.
A Customer Data Platform helps businesses collect user-specific by integrating different sources to form a 360-degree view of a user. That information is further sliced and diced for segmentation and campaign management.
